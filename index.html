Hi I'm David and I am a Technical Designer

  I am a Technical Designer who focuses on communication and implementation of mechanics across teams. I enjoy tackling the problem of how to get a design that feels good and then getting that design implemented. I also love to see players engaging with that design and then taking any feedback to tweak the design or implementation until it feels just right.


  I recently graduated from the University of California: Santa Cruz with a Computer Science: Game Design degree. This means that I have extensive experience working with the design and implementation of mechanics into any project I am working on. I am also well versed in working in teams and communicating the implementation of a game to non-programmer teammates. I work to ensure that the mechanics of the project correctly convey the intended design and to improve cohesion between the mechanics and the aesthetic of the game.


  I am currently leading a team working on a a team-based cryptid game.
