Growing Gardens

Role

Lead Designer

Technology Used

Tabletopia

Adobe Illustrator

Team Size

1 person

I managed every aspect about Growing Gardens. I designed the mechanics from the ground up and designed each card. I also organized playtesting sessions to collect data on how the game played. I used the collected data to iterate upon my design and make the mechanics and art better. One example of this iteration was my introduction of a mechanic to remove unneccesary resources from the board so players could rapidly reduce the amount of resources they had to match their goal.
